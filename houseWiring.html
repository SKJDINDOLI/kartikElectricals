House Wiring

We are a fully licensed and insured electrical contractor specializing in residential house wiring. Our team of experienced electricians are dedicated to providing top-quality service and ensuring the safety and satisfaction of our clients.

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • New construction wiring
  • Remodeling and renovation wiring
  • Panel upgrades and replacements
  • Generator installation
  • Smoke and carbon monoxide detector installation
  • Outdoor lighting and landscape lighting
  • Home automation and smart home integration

Our team is equipped with the latest technology and tools to ensure that all of our work is up to code and meets the highest industry standards. We take pride in our attention to detail and strive to leave your home with a clean and organized work area.

We understand that electrical work can be stressful and overwhelming, which is why we take the time to explain every step of the process to our clients and ensure that all of their questions and concerns are addressed.
